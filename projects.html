Proud to Partner with Some of Wyoming’s Finest



"You hear it all the time, human capital is the key. Our solution? Elevate Wyoming! Elevate has become a part of our Jonah family and we benefit daily from their contributions."
Kim DeVore, CFO

Jonah Bank

Jonah Bank of Wyoming is a local bank that is deeply committed to its local communities. Early adopters of the Code of the West, they also believe that culture is a critical aspect of their bank’s success. Having grown radically and expanded to four branches, they wanted to be as talented at developing people as they are in the world of finance. We helped them create a Legacy Leadership program that honored the Jonah Bank Code and tied it to daily behaviors. This program was initially delivered to managers and has expanded to all employees.

"Too often strategic plans are made and go into a binder and put on a shelf where they collect dust. The strategic plan that Elevate helped CHCCW build is a living, breathing plan that is designed to be used daily and updated as needed."
Cole White, CEO

Community Health Centers of Central Wyoming

Community Health Centers of Central Wyoming believes in providing quality, affordable healthcare to the community. Creating the best possible experience for patients means doing the same for its employees. They wanted to mobilize their senior leaders and mobilize their people to return to their mission. We worked with them to determine where their employees were and how they could better engage them. Through a collaborative process, we crafted a strategic plan that was more than a one-time creation. Monthly sessions celebrate the regular progress they’re making towards that plan’s goals and also devote resources to further developing their senior leadership team.

"I reached out for executive coaching to help organize our multi-clinic business structure - coordinating multiple directors and keeping everyone focused on the same mission, vision and goals for the individual clinics and the company as a whole. Elevate Wyoming has helped us in these areas and we are moving solidly forward with a structure everyone can work with to succeed."
Kathy Blair, Owner

Wind City Physical Therapy

Wind City Physical Therapy has locations across the State and believes it’s possible to truly serve patients and run a healthy human-centered business. They wanted to reinforce their front-line employees’ value to the organization and enlist them in creating the best possible experience for their patients. We helped them build an interactive series of development sessions, so front-line staff could provide quality patient care and run day-to-day operations more efficiently. We then worked with their clinic Directors to refine some of their management systems and processes. As they continue to grow, we’ll continue to work with the owners to ensure that growth stays in line with their enduring vision.

"We consider this work with our employees to be one of the agency's highest priorities. After months of interviews, we selected Elevate Wyoming because of their sincere passion to do what's right for our agency and our mission."
John Kennedy, Deputy Director

Wyoming Game and Fish

The Wyoming Game and Fish Department has tremendous pride and confidence in the agency's culture and believes that developing employees’ leadership skills will make their culture, commitment, and performance even stronger, more sustainable. They believe that building individual, managerial, and organizational leadership skills will help them perform individual duties better, communicate and collaborate with others more effectively, and forge a better future for us all. We helped them determine needs across the department, design a hands-on curriculum employees will embrace, and develop highly interactive two-day sessions held in various locations around the State. The result is a tiered Leadership Development program that focuses on personal leadership the first-year and a second-year program designed for people in informal and formal leadership roles.
